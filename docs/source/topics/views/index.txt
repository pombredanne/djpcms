.. module:: djpcms.views

=================
View handlers
=================

As soon as a client request a url, the primary job of ``djpcms`` is to find and invoke
a ``view handler``. These view handlers are instances of
:class:`djpcms.views.baseview.djpcmsview` the ``djpcms`` view base class.
